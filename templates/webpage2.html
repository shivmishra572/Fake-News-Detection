About Us

The boundless spread of misinformation can have quite contradictory aftermaths. It can spread hatred and confusion amongst people and sometimes also cause riots in the countries. Fake news can mislead people to a great extent. Fake news is nothing but a piece of false information that is often misleading and generated with an intent to damage the reputation of a person or organization or to make money with advertising revenue.
So we present "Fakebox" so that you can detect fake news with the help of artificial intelligence. We are implementing a natural language processing algorithm to predict whether the text related to a piece of news is fake or not.

How to spot a Fake news!

SUPPORTING SOURCES

Click on links or check the official sources. Do they support the story?

CONSIDER THE SOURCE

Is there an author? Check out the credentials on relevant issues.

ASK THE EXPERTS

Ask a librarian, or consult a fact-checking site, official sources.

READ BEYOND
